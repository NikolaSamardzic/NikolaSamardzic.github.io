S.School

S.School is an online school that offers a wide range of personalised learning programmes and courses. Although S.School is an online school, you won’t feel alone. You will get the opportunity to interact with other students at event days and subject days. You’ll also share and exchange ideas with your teachers and fellow students through discussions on S.SchooolMeet, our online learning platform.

Our teachers will stay in close contact with you, including ongoing face-to-face meetings if you need it.

Students of all ages are welcome!
